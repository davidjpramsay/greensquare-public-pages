Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 May 2026

GreenSquare Times Tables is an iPad educational app developed by David Ramsay. This app is designed for offline multiplication practice and local progress tracking.

Information the app stores

The app stores the following information locally on the device:

  • Adventurer profile names
  • Per-adventurer game settings
  • Per-adventurer journey and completion history

Information the app does not collect

  • No account registration data
  • No analytics or advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • No tracking across apps or websites

How data is used

The locally stored information is used only to operate the app features, including profile selection, saved settings, and progress history.

Data sharing

GreenSquare Times Tables does not sell personal data and does not share app data with third-party advertisers or analytics providers.

Children and classroom use

The app is intended for educational use. The current app version does not require online accounts and does not include social features, messaging, advertising, or external tracking.
